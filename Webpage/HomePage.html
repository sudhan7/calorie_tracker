Main Contributions

We build highly accurate machine-learning models to predict the type, size and calories of a food item based on its image. We build training datasets for these three supervised learning tasks mentioned above that will be made publicly available and can be further used by the research community.

Easy to work with

Project Summary

Calculating the amount of calories in a given food item is now a common task. We propose a machine-learning-based approach to predict the amount of calories from food images. Our system does not require any input from the user, except from an image of the food item.
